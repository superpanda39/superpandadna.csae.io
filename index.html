BlisSoirée Child Safety Standards

At BlisSoirée, protecting children from sexual abuse, exploitation, and harmful content is a top priority. We maintain a zero-tolerance policy toward Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) and are committed to providing a safe online environment for all users.

1. Zero-Tolerance Policy

BlisSoirée strictly prohibits:

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Child exploitation content
  • Grooming or predatory behavior
  • Sexualization of minors
  • Solicitation involving minors
  • Human trafficking involving minors
  • Any content that promotes, depicts, encourages, or facilitates child abuse or exploitation

Any violation of this policy may result in immediate account suspension or permanent removal from the platform.

2. User Reporting

Users can report suspected CSAE content, behavior, or accounts through in-app reporting tools or by contacting our moderation team at vrob0927@gmail.com.

Reports are reviewed as quickly as possible and prioritized when child safety concerns are involved.

3. Content Moderation

BlisSoirée employs moderation measures that may include:

  • Automated detection systems
  • User-generated reports
  • Manual review by moderators
  • Account investigations

Content that violates this policy will be removed immediately upon confirmation.

4. Account Enforcement

BlisSoirée reserves the right to:

  • Remove offending content
  • Suspend accounts
  • Permanently ban users
  • Restrict platform access
  • Preserve evidence for law enforcement investigations where legally required

No warnings are required before enforcement actions are taken for CSAE violations.

5. Cooperation with Law Enforcement

BlisSoirée will cooperate with appropriate law enforcement agencies and child protection organizations when legally required. Where applicable, credible reports involving child sexual abuse or exploitation may be reported to relevant authorities and organizations responsible for child protection.

6. Age Restrictions

BlisSoirée is intended only for users who meet the minimum age requirements established by applicable laws and platform policies.

Users may not:

  • Misrepresent their age
  • Create accounts for minors without appropriate authorization
  • Use the platform to contact, exploit, or target minors

7. Safety by Design

BlisSoirée is committed to continuously improving safety measures, including:

  • Abuse reporting mechanisms
  • Content moderation procedures
  • Community guidelines enforcement
  • Risk assessment and prevention measures

8. Community Responsibility

All users are expected to help maintain a safe community by:

  • Reporting suspicious activity
  • Following community guidelines
  • Respecting the rights and safety of others

Failure to comply may result in enforcement action.
